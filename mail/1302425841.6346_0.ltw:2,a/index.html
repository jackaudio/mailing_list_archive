There is a JackRouter.ini (text file) that defines wanted in/out channels number. Just edit and adapt it *before* launching any Jack client.

[IO]
input=4  		<== put 6 here
output=4  	<== put 6 here

[AUTO_CONNECT]
input=1
output=1
