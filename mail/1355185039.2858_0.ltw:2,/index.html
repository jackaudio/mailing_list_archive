> I also have an Echo Gina24 ... Even when the card seemed to be fully
> functional, sometimes when I was listening really close, I heard strange
> artifacts, like digital distortion. The really best result was ardour
> playback with some default kernel, that seemed to be smooth, but otherwise
> .. dont know, the card got possibly old ...

It sounds like it was suffering from digital noise getting into the analog
circuits.  This could be radiated by nearby cards or cables, or even
mainboard settings (clock spread spectrum settings for example).  Since the
card is old, there may even be an issue with some filter capacitors which
are making it more susceptable to noise pickup.

> Also, I'm not 100% happy with the Gina's noise level. I can hear my
> CPU/GPU when on maximum level, .. guess that can be filtered out if I use
> the ADAT optical interfaces tho. ...

Again, this sounds like radiated noise getting into the analog circuits.  If
the ADAT interfaces are used this won't be a problem.
