"By default, JackRouter is set to provide 4 Input and 4 Output
connections. This setting can be customized to suite your needs by editing
the JackRouter.ini file within your C:\Program Files\Jack\32bits (or
C:\Program Files\Jack\64bits folder depending of the JackRouter 32 or 64
bit version). In Windows Explorer, right click on the file and choose the
'Edit' option. Change the settings in the [IO] portion of the file."
